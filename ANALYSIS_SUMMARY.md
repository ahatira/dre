## Key Findings from Mockups

### 12 Homepage Sections Identified
1. **Header/Navigation** - Logo, menu, search, CTA, language selector
2. **Hero Section** - "What are you looking for?" with search widget
3. **Services Grid** - 4 service cards (Expertise, Network, Solutions, Innovation)
4. **Approach Section** - Accordion with approach questions
5. **Featured Properties** - Carousel of 4 property cards
6. **Commercial Searches** - 4 commercial search category cards
7. **Expert Support** - Media object with team photo and benefits list
8. **News Articles** - 3 article cards with dates and descriptions
9. **Market Studies** - 2 study cards with download CTAs
10. **FAQ Accordion** - Multiple Q&A items (expandable)
11. **Data Table** - Statistics/market data
12. **Footer** - Multi-column layout with links and contact info

### Components Most Frequently Used
- **Card** - Properties, articles, services, studies (5+ use cases)
- **Grid** - Multiple column layouts (4-col, 3-col, 2-col responsive)
- **Button** - CTAs in every section
- **Accordion** - Approach section + FAQ
- **Icon** - Service cards, navigation, metadata

### Design System Extracted
- **Colors**: Green primary (#00915A), Pink secondary (#A12B66)
- **Typography**: Bold sans-serif headings, regular body text
- **Spacing**: 3-4x line height between sections, 20px card padding, 20-24px grid gaps
- **Responsive**: Desktop 4-col → Tablet 3-col → Mobile 1-2 col
